Finance app as it should always have been

If all you want to know is how much money you have to spend/save in a month. That's the app for you !!!

You don't need to become an accountant to manage your own money.

Recording every penny spent and classifiy every category is not your thing?
This app will do everything for you automatically. It will get all your accounts, credit-cards, monthly bills, debt payments, read all your extracts and update you. No more login, logout and manual compilation of data from all sort of sources and banks and accounts.

The information you need to make your money last!

The app does classifies your expenses in only two types: unavoidable and all others. Unavoidable expenses are the basic ones you need for living (rent, fuel, energy bill, supermarket food...). All the others are things that you could choose not to spend or spend less. Things like Restaurants, Clothing , Weekend travel, hangging out...
Under those two, it then calculates the expenses yet to come (debt payment, credit card purchases), your income, what you spent so far and then gives you how much money you have left, if any, to be positive in the rest of the month.
You can then decide what will you do with that money. Save for a new car? Travel this weekend?
